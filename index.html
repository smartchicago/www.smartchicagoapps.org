About

The Smart Chicago Collaborative offers civic developers in Chicago free access to Amazon Web Services and Heroku for hosting their applications.

If you are a developer and are interested in free hosting for your application, please complete the sign up form.

You can see a number of projects that Smart Chicago maintains and supports at the Smart Chicago Github page.
